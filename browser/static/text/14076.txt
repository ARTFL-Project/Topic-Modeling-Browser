This play is Paris , 1 janvier 1778 . . . plus intéressant qu'Agatocle et par conséquent susceptible d'un plus grand succès, quoique peut-être écrite d'une manière un peu moins soignée. [Voltaire had not wished to break away from the rule requiring the reading of plays to the actors, for fear of antagonizing them. Irène would be read by Monvel 1 , Lekain having been unable to undertake the task. The actors would thus get to know the play, but they would not discuss it. As soon as it had been approved by the police] on procédera à la copie des rôles et à leur distribution.
