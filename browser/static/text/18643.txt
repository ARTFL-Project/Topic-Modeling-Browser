Editorial comment : He has taken milk, which has disagreed with him. The Dutch newspapers have printed a statement, attributed to the Dutch ambassador in Paris, that Voltaire's arrest has been ordered. For the past month the newspapers have been writing about him, and everybody wants to meet him. He has been to Amsterdam. He is in despair about all these statements. He still intends to print the Éléments de la philosophie de Newton in Holland; it will be known that he is there, if it be not already known, and this will at least show for what reason, which can only do good.
