Editorial comment — He has been seriously ill, and cannot better employ the first moments of his convalescence than to carry out the duty imposed on him by the most entire and respectful gratitude. Paris , 31 décembre 1774 . . . Ma faible voix ne peut rien ajouter à votre gloire. L'Europe entière a couronné vos travaux de lauriers qui ne se faneront jamais.] Nevertheless he hopes that Voltaire's health will enable him to write a few more tragedies in which he himself could be employed before his retirement] qui s'avance de jour en jour. [He begs Voltaire not to abandon the theatre in its deplorable condition.] Vous soutiendrez encore en public le bon goût que toutes les fadaises dramatiques font disparaître très sensiblement. Rendez vous aux vœux du public éclairé et sensible. [Refers to Argental and his sorrow on the death of his wife.]
