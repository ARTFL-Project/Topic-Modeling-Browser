Editorial comment — He thanks Palissot for his Eloge de Voltaire 2 . 28 avril 1778 . . . Vous m'avez rendu justice, en croyant que je recevrai avec enthousiasme ces témoignages de vos sentiments pour lui. [He applauds the hommage rendered to a great man whom they all love and admire. He regrets the literary quarrels which are now more envenomed than ever. They divide men of letters, and he sees no hope until a peace of Westphalia puts an end to them.]
