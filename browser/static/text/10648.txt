Editorial comment — He supposes that Frederick is about to set off to undertake a siege; sends him changes in the ode on Wilhelmina of Bayreuth, asks for permission to print it and to add some prose to it; tells him that the Histoire de Brandebourg has been attributed to the writer. [ c . 1 March 1759] . . . J'ai désavoué et je désavouerai à jamais cette imposture. [Tells Frederick that pamphlets are being published against him.]
