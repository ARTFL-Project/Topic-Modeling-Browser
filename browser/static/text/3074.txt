Editorial comment : He has devoured with his eyes the first volume of the Siècle de Louis XIV which he saw in the hands of mme Denis. He fears that notwithstanding all the precautions that have been taken copies will reach Paris or some other place, and be at once printed. This book has made an extraordinary impression in Paris, everybody wants it and many have written to Berlin or Dresden to have whatever is in print at any price. Paris , 14 novembre 1751 . . . Vos imprimeurs sont ils plus fidèles que ceux de Paris? J'ai bien de la peine à le croire, et la friponnerie typographique est une suite du péché originel que la régénération baptismale n'efface que difficilement. Prenez y bien garde. . . .
