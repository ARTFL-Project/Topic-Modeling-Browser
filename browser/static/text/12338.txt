Editorial comment : This excerpt is taken from an early 20th-century biography of madame de Graffigny. 28 avril 1739 . . . Avez vous un peu ouï parler de mon mémoire sur le feu? Je ne ferais point cette question là à une autre, mais j'espère que votre amitié la permet. . . . [She fears the effects that may be caused by the copies in circulation of the epistles on man, and on pleasure, and of the writings addressed by Voltaire to mme de Graffigny and Desmarets. She asks her to maintain in Paris the acquaintance made at Cirey with her brother the abbé de Breteuil.]
